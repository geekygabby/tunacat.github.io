What is TunaCat?

At TunaCat, we understand that your privacy matters! This site was created with the goal of making online security and simple and accessible for everyone. We provide clear, up-to-date information about the risks that come with surfing the web, along with the practical tools and tips that are essential in helping you stay protected. Whether or not you are a gal who likes to casually broswe the web or a tech enthusiast, we are here to encourage you to take control of your digital life! So, let's stay private and stay safe!

Key Terms:

Term Definition
Authentication Cookies Save a user's information when they log into a website.
Tracking Cookies Record your web usage and save information from your time on the website.
First-Party Cookies Cookies created and stored by the website you are directly visiting to improve user experience.
Third-Party Cookies Cookies placed by a different domain than the one you are visiting, often used for advertising and tracking across sites.
Cookie Consent Banner A pop-up or notice that informs users about cookies used on the website and requests their permission to proceed.
Browser Fingerprinting A technique to track users by collecting unique information about their device and browser settings without using cookies.
Privacy Sandbox An initiative by Google to develop new web technologies that protect privacy while allowing advertisers to deliver relevant ads without third-party cookies.
Contextual Advertising Displaying ads based on the content of the current webpage, rather than tracking a user's past behavior.
First-Party Data Information collected directly from users by a website or company, such as email addresses, preferences, and purchases.
Server-Side Tagging A method of collecting and processing user data on a server instead of directly on the user's browser, enhancing privacy control.
