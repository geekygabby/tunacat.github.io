What is TunaCat?

This website was made with the goal of teaching anyone who is on the internet how to protect themselves from data mining and information spoofing, specifically when it comes to cookies.

Key Terms:

Authentication Cookies: save a user's information when they log into a website.

Tracking Cookies: records your web usage and saves information from your time on the website.
