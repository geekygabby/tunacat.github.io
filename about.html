What is TunaCat?

At TunaCat, we understand that your privacy matters! This site was created with the goal of making online security and simple and accessible for everyone. We provide clear, up-to-date information about the risks that come with surfing the web, along with the practical tools and tips that are essential in helping you stay protected. Whether or not you are a gal who likes to casually broswe the web or a tech enthusiast, we are here to encourage you to take control of your digital life! So, let's stay private and stay safe!

Key Terms:

Term Definition
Authentication Cookies Save a user's information when they log into a website.
Tracking Cookies Record your web usage and save information from your time on the website.
