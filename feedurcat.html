Protect yourself from cookies!

Your computer's browser is probably full of cookies, and companies use that information to advertise to you. This is just one aspect of protecting your privacy. Companies still use non-cookie alternatives to track you across websites and even clearing your cookies cannot stop that. By changing your settings you can protect yourself while browsing. There are first-party cookies and third-party cookies.

First-party cookies are what we have been talking about this whole time, what the site initially uses to track you.
